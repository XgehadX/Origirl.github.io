ABOUT US

Origami is very useful for children because it teaches them many things, including emotional stability because it requires intense concentration to fold the paper and encourages logical thinking and helps in planning and concentration and requires good control of the fingers to fold the paper, which improves children's motor skills. It is also a good way to reduce stress and anxiety because children use it to relax and meditate and it helps in calming the mind. It is also not just an art, but it can be used to teach children geometry, mathematics and shapes through origami and it can be used in designing engineering structures.
